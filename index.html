The Problem

Token-weighted governance is broken by design.

Most DAOs hand governance power to whoever holds the most tokens — a system that rewards capital accumulation, not community contribution. The loudest voices aren't the most engaged members; they're the wealthiest wallets.

Existing "proof-of-participation" solutions issue badges as off-chain trophies with zero governance weight. You show up, you get a PNG, and then someone with 10 million tokens outvotes your entire community. Participation becomes theater.

Meanwhile, Sybil attacks remain trivial. Without KYC or meaningful onboarding, one actor can spin up hundreds of wallets, collect badges freely, and dominate any vote. The communities that care most about integrity have no viable path to fair governance.

Plutocracy Masquerading as Democracy

Token-gated voting concentrates power with VCs and early holders, not active participants who built the community.

Badges With No Teeth

POAP and similar systems create engagement records that are entirely disconnected from decision-making power.

Sybil Attacks at Scale

Without identity verification, fake wallets freely farm participation badges and corrupt governance outcomes.

The Solution

Attendance becomes authority.

Rootcast wires on-chain attendance directly into governance weight. Every verified action — attending a Space, completing a bounty, being vouched by existing members — mints a compressed NFT via Metaplex Bubblegum and increments your non-transferable Presence Score stored in a Program Derived Address.

That Presence Score isn't cosmetic. Our Anchor-based governance program reads it directly when weighing votes. Recent participation counts more than old badges thanks to configurable decay curves set by community admins. Show up consistently, govern meaningfully.

Sybil resistance comes from a social vouching graph: three existing members with minimum Presence Scores must vouch for any new wallet, creating a network of accountability without KYC. Every score, every vote, every vouching record is fully auditable on-chain through Helius DAS.

How It Works

From attendance to governance in four steps.

01
Community Admin

Configure Your Community

Admins define event types, set Presence Score decay curves, configure quorum thresholds, and establish minimum vouching scores — all through the Next.js UI with zero Rust required. Parameters are written to the Anchor program on-chain.

02
Member Action

Participate & Claim Attestations

Members attend Spaces, complete bounties, or get vouched by peers. Each action generates a time-locked claim link. Members connect their wallet, claim the cNFT, and their Presence Score increments automatically on-chain.

03
Sybil Resistance

Build Your Vouching Graph

New community members need three vouches from existing members who've crossed the minimum Presence Score threshold. Each vouch writes a social graph edge to a PDA, creating an accountable web of on-chain identity.

04
Governance

Vote With Earned Weight

When proposals go live, the Anchor governance program tallies votes weighted by each wallet's Presence Score. Results finalize on-chain, Dialect sends notifications, and every outcome is publicly auditable via Helius DAS.
